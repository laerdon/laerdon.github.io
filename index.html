Laerdon Kim - Undergraduate Researcher at Cornell University

Hi! I'm Laerdon Kim, a freshman at Cornell University studying Computer Science and Mathematics. I'm interested in NLP for long-term social/scientific collaboration with AI agents, investigating language model embeddings, and neuro-symbolic ML for common-sense reasoning. My research advisors are Professor Cristian Danescu-Niculescu-Mizil and Professor Lillian Lee.
